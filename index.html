Cool Facts about Belarus

No Sea Coast

Belarus is the largest country in Europe without access to the sea!

Forests & Swamps

Over 35% of the land is covered by forests, with swamps taking up a significant part.

Bison in Białowieża

The Białowieża Forest hosts the largest population of European bison.

IT Hub

Belarus is a true IT paradise, home to projects like World of Tanks and Viber.

Interesting Denomination

In 2016, the Belarusian ruble was re-denominated by cutting 4 zeros for easier calculations.

Countless Rivers & Lakes

There are over 20,000 rivers and more than 10,000 lakes throughout the country!

Bilingual Nation

Both Belarusian and Russian are official languages, creating a unique cultural blend.

Unique Souvenirs

Traditional motifs and crafts make Belarusian souvenirs truly one-of-a-kind!

A Bit of History

Belarus has an ancient history dating back to the times of Kievan Rus. This land has witnessed the rise and fall of mighty principalities and left a rich legacy in architecture, literature, and traditions.

Nature of Belarus

From picturesque forests to endless lakes and rivers, the nature of Belarus captivates with its diversity. Visit Białowieża and enjoy the fresh air and serene atmosphere.
